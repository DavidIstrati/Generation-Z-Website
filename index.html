About Us

Generation Z is a project created exclusively by high school students. We regularly organise events with celebrity speakers, the topics ranging from history and art to cosmonauts and refugees. Our goal is to create direct dialogue between students and leaders from different fields, in order to help the new generation better understand the word they live in. Every event is closed with a Q&A session and pictures with the speaker!
