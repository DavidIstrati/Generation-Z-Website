From Mishu to Manhattan

Vlad Dragusin

During the November event, students from all over the country had the opportunity to talk to Vlad Dragusin, a former CNMV student who, in the meantime, founded the Candylab company in New York. He introduced us to the company, the laboratory, the player models, he talked about the difficulties he went through and about his life as an entrepreneur.

Vanity Publishing - do you really know how to pick your books? (remote)

Bogdan Hrib

Our first event this season had the founder of the Tritonic publishing house as a guest speaker. In a discussion about ‘'vanity publishing”, our students also learnt about the Romanian authors in countries such as the UK and the USA or how to publish their own literary works.
Zero Gravity

Dumitru Prunariu

The first and only Romanian cosmonaut, Dumitru Prunariu told us his unique story: the behind-the-scenes and aftermath of a great experience. We understood that life is unpredictable, but we must not give up.
Undestanding the refugee's challanges

Nisreen Rubaian

It was a great honour to have Nisreen Rubaian, the special United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees representative in Romania, who clarified the term ‘’refugee’' and made us more aware of the current critical situation.
Who do you want to become?

Adrian Titieni

Our first special guest was the actor Adrian Titieni. A bold speech about staying true to yourself followed by a Q&A session, this event helped the teenagers get one step closer to finding themselves.
